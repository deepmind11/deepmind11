### About Me

I build production-grade bioinformatics infrastructure that turns raw sequencing reads into actionable clinical insight.

Currently at **BillionToOne** (Menlo Park, CA), I work on Nextflow pipelines processing **2,000+ patient samples per week** for oncology molecular diagnostics. Previously, I built automated genomics workflows at **Columbia University** and prescriptive ML models at **Standard Chartered Bank**.

My background spans pipeline engineering, cloud infrastructure, variant calling, and multi-omics analysis. I hold an **MA in Biotechnology from Columbia University** (GPA 3.80) and a **BS in Chemistry from IIT Kanpur**.

Right now, I'm exploring how **multi-agent AI systems** can automate bioinformatics workflows: triaging QC failures, reasoning over experimental context, and orchestrating downstream analysis.
